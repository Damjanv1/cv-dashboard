Methodology. Data pulled live from your browser, straight from the Lichess public API (/api/games/user/…) — no server involved, nothing is stored. Ratings shown are post-game (pre-game + rating change). "Decisive win %" = wins ÷ (wins + losses), excluding draws. Material at end-of-game computed by simulating SAN moves with chess.js in a background worker; values are standard (P=1, N=B=3, R=5, Q=9). ECO family derives from the opening's ECO-code letter.
